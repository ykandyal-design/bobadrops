lorelai‚Äôs cinnamon latte

approved by the expert herself

ingredients:

  • 2 shots espresso
  • 1 cup steamed milk
  • 1 tbsp cinnamon syrup
  • whipped cream + a pinch of cinnamon

instructions:

  1. brew espresso shots.
  2. steam milk until frothy.
  3. mix espresso with cinnamon syrup.
  4. pour milk gently, top with whipped cream & sprinkle cinnamon.
  5. enjoy!

pumpkin mocha special

rory's choice (and yashvi's choice)

ingredients:

  • 1 shot espresso
  • 1 tbsp pumpkin puree
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar or maple syrup
  • whipped cream + cinnamon for topping

instructions:

  1. heat milk with pumpkin, cocoa, and sugar until warm but not boiling.
  2. brew espresso shot.
  3. combine espresso and pumpkin milk mixture.
  4. top with whipped cream and sprinkle cinnamon.
  5. enjoy while watching the show!
